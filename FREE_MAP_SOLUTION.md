## 💡 Technical Details

### What is OpenStreetMap?
- Free, open-source map data
- Maintained by a community of mappers
- Used by major companies (Apple, Facebook, etc.)
- Covers the entire world

### What is Leaflet?
- Popular JavaScript library for interactive maps
- Lightweight and fast
- Mobile-friendly
- Used by thousands of websites

### How It Loads:
1. When you click "Pick on Map", it loads Leaflet library from CDN
2. Displays OpenStreetMap tiles (map images)
3. Creates an interactive map with a draggable marker
4. Saves the location as a Google Maps link (for compatibility)
